🌙 Credit System

How Olive Credits Work

What are Olive Credits?

Olive Credits are your access key to the villa.

  • ✓ 1 credit = 1 person / 1 night (baseline)
  • ✓ Credits are earned yearly based on your shares
  • ✓ 1 full tree = 4 credits/year

Seasonal Pricing

Season Cost per Person/Night
Low Season 1 credit
Mid Season 1.5 credits
High Season 2–3 credits

Example:

2 people, mid-season:
→ 2 × 1.5 = 3 credits/night

Important Notes

  • • Credits expire after 24–36 months
  • • Peak dates are limited and competitive
  • • Booking is subject to availability
