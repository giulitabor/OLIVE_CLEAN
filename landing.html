What Are Olive Credits?

Olive Credits (also called Residency Credits) are your villa access currency. They let you book nights at the Olivium Estate — a private Tuscan villa shared only by members.

🌿 1 Credit = 1 Night

For up to 2 guests (baseline). Additional guests cost 0.5 credits per night each.

  • Seasonal multipliers apply (1–3 credits per night)
  • Issued annually based on your tier
  • Managed via your Olive Credits wallet in the app

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Built for Groups

Our hybrid system makes it easy to bring family and friends.

  • Up to 2 guests → 1 credit/night (base)
  • Each extra guest → 0.5 credits/night
  • Max 12 guests at the villa

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my family?

Yes! Our hybrid system is designed for families and groups. Up to 2 guests are included in the base credit rate; extra guests cost 0.5 credits per night each.

Can I combine credits across years?

No — credits expire after 24–36 months. Plan your stays within that window.

Can I sell or transfer my credits?

No. Credits are non-transferable and personal to the member.

What if I run out of credits?

You can top up with cash at the member rate. Contact concierge for availability.

How do I earn harvest credits?

Attend the annual olive harvest (October–December) and you'll receive +3 credits added to your wallet. It's a hands-on estate experience!

How do social credits work?

Share photos or videos of your Olivium experience on social media with #OliviumEstate. Each approved post earns +0.5 credits (max 2 credits/year).

How do I book?

Through the Olivium app — check your wallet, view the calendar, and reserve your dates.

Is the villa private?

Yes. The villa is for members only — no public bookings, no hotel-style operations.
