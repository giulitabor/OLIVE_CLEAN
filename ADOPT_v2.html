Full Disclosure & Privacy

[ CLOSE_ESC ]

1. RWA Protocol Disclaimer

Olivium is a decentralized protocol facilitating the fractionalization of agricultural assets located in San Vincenzo, Tuscany. Ownership of OVL tokens represents a contractual interest in the yield and utility of physical assets (Olea europaea). This is not an investment in a regulated security. Participation involves risks including crop failure, regional weather anomalies, and smart contract vulnerabilities.

2. On-Chain vs. Cadastral Reality

While token balances are maintained on the Solana blockchain, the ultimate legal title is anchored in the Italian Land Registry (Catasto). The DAO operates via a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) that bridges digital ownership to physical land rights. In the event of a total network failure, the SPV maintains a physical ledger of all holders as of the last finalized block.

3. Privacy & Data Protection (GDPR 2026)

We adhere to "Privacy by Design." Your wallet address is considered personal data under GDPR. We do not link wallet addresses to real-world identities (KYC) unless required for Villa stay bookings or legal distributions exceeding €10,000. We use Helius RPC nodes which may log IP addresses for anti-DDoS purposes; these logs are purged every 24 hours.

4. Operational Disclosures

Villa Credits are non-transferable and must be used within the calendar year. The "Guardian" status requires a minimum stake of 1,000 OVL. Harvesting cycles occur between October and December annually; yield distributions are finalized on-chain within 14 business days of pressing.
