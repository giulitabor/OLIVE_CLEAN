Full Disclosure

1. RWA Operational Reality

Olivium tokenizes Real World Assets (RWA). Ownership of OVL or Tree NFTs corresponds to a membership interest in the harvest and utility of a physical estate in San Vincenzo, Tuscany. Unlike pure digital assets, these are tied to seasonal cycles and Italian agricultural laws.

2. Legal Structure (Cadastral)

Digital shares are legally recognized through a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) registered in Italy. While the Solana blockchain serves as the source of truth for membership, the physical asset is protected by the Italian Land Registry (Catasto).

3. Data & Privacy (GDPR)

We maintain a "Pseudonymous-First" protocol. Wallet addresses are logged only to verify access rights via Helius RPC nodes. Personal identity data is only required during physical residency check-ins at the villa, in compliance with Italian tourism regulations.
