Section 1

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13 July 2026

Olivium ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website, use our mobile application, or engage with our membership services.

Data Controller: Olivium Estate · San Vincenzo, Tuscany, Italy
Contact: privacy@olivium.io
Data Protection Officer (DPO): Available at dpo@olivium.io

1.1 Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity Data: Full name, date of birth, nationality, passport or ID number (for residency verification).
  • Contact Data: Email address, postal address, telephone number.
  • Membership Data: Membership tier, tree assignments, olive allocations, residency credits, booking history.
  • Payment Data: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through PCI-compliant third parties).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, usage data from our website and app.
  • Marketing & Communications Data: Your preferences for receiving marketing communications from us.

1.2 How We Use Your Data

We process your personal data for the following purposes, relying on one or more lawful bases under the GDPR:

  • Membership Administration: To manage your membership, allocate trees, process olive oil distributions, and provide member services. (Contractual necessity)
  • Residency Bookings: To process stay reservations, handle check-in/out, and provide concierge services. (Contractual necessity)
  • Communication: To send administrative emails, membership updates, harvest notifications, and respond to inquiries. (Legitimate interest)
  • Marketing: To inform you about estate events, new offerings, and member experiences — only where you have consented or where permitted by law. (Consent / Legitimate interest)
  • Security & Compliance: To protect our systems, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations. (Legal obligation / Legitimate interest)

1.3 Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing necessary for the performance of your membership agreement.
  • Legal Obligation: Processing required to comply with Italian and EU legal requirements.
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing necessary for our legitimate business interests, provided these do not override your rights.
  • Consent: Processing based on your explicit consent (e.g., marketing communications).

1.4 Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, and reporting requirements. Generally:

  • Active Memberships: Data retained for the duration of your membership plus 7 years for legal and tax purposes.
  • Inactive Memberships: Data retained for 3 years after membership termination unless you request deletion.
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you withdraw consent or unsubscribe.

1.5 Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, IT service providers, email delivery services, and estate management partners — all contractually bound to process data only on our instructions.
  • Legal Authorities: Where required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Successors: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

1.6 International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). Where such transfers occur, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

1.7 Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): Request deletion of your data where no legal obligation requires retention.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of how we process your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another controller.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@olivium.io. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante per la protezione dei dati personali).

1.8 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include encryption, access controls, secure hosting, and regular security assessments.

1.9 Children's Data

Olivium does not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18 years of age. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

Section 3

Membership Agreement

This Membership Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Olivium Estate ("Olivium") and you ("Member") upon acceptance of a membership tier.

3.1 Membership Obligations

  • Stewardship: Members acknowledge that membership represents stewardship of the estate, not legal ownership of land or trees.
  • Annual Renewal: Memberships renew automatically on the anniversary of the enrolment date unless cancelled in accordance with the Cancellation Policy.
  • Fees: All membership fees are payable in advance and are non-refundable except as expressly provided in the Refund Policy.

3.2 Membership Benefits

  • Tree Stewardship: Members receive stewardship of designated olive trees as per their selected tier.
  • Olive Oil Allocation: Annual allocation of extra virgin olive oil, as specified in the tier description.
  • Residency Credits: Annual accommodation credits as per tier, subject to the Residency & Booking Policy.
  • Booking Priority: Members receive booking windows according to their tier.

3.3 Tree Replacement

If a stewarded tree naturally declines, dies, or becomes unproductive, Olivium will replace it with another healthy tree of similar age and quality. Members will be notified of any such replacement.

3.4 Transferability

Memberships are non-transferable except with Olivium's prior written consent, which may be withheld at our sole discretion. Upon death of a member, the membership may be transferred to a designated beneficiary.

3.5 Termination

  • By Member: Members may terminate membership by providing written notice at least 30 days before the renewal date.
  • By Olivium: We reserve the right to terminate membership for breach of these terms, illegal activity, or conduct detrimental to the community.

Section 4

Residency & Booking Policy

4.1 Residency Credits

  • 1 Residency Credit = 1 complimentary overnight stay in designated Olivium accommodation.
  • Credits are allocated annually at membership renewal.
  • Credits may be used consecutively for extended stays or divided across multiple visits.
  • Credits have no cash value and cannot be redeemed for monetary refunds.

4.2 Booking Process

  • Bookings must be made through the Olivium app or member portal.
  • Booking windows are determined by membership tier (higher tiers receive priority).
  • All reservations are subject to availability.
  • Members may book accommodation for themselves, family, and invited guests (within maximum occupancy).

4.3 Cancellation & No-Show

  • Cancellations made more than 30 days before arrival: credits will be returned to your account.
  • Cancellations made 14–30 days before arrival: 50% of credits deducted.
  • Cancellations made less than 14 days before arrival: 100% of credits forfeited.
  • No-shows will result in forfeiture of all credits for that booking.
  • Exemption requests will be considered on a compassionate basis.

4.4 Guest Responsibility

  • Members are responsible for the conduct of their guests.
  • Damage to estate property will be charged to the member's account.
  • Maximum occupancy limits apply and must not be exceeded.

4.5 Check-in / Check-out

  • Check-in: 3:00 PM – 8:00 PM (late arrival by prior arrangement).
  • Check-out: 10:00 AM.
  • Late check-out may be available upon request (subject to availability).

Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Olivium uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and mobile application.

5.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and improve your experience.

5.2 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Necessary for core functionality, including authentication, security, and session management. Cannot be disabled.

Examples: session_id, auth_token

Preference Cookies

Remember your preferences such as language selection, font size, and region.

Examples: lang, region, theme

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (anonymous aggregate data).

Examples: _ga, _gid (Google Analytics)

Marketing Cookies

Track your activity to deliver relevant advertising and measure campaign effectiveness.

Examples: _fbp, _gcl_au

5.3 Your Consent

We obtain your consent for non-essential cookies through our cookie banner. You can manage your preferences at any time through your browser settings or our cookie preferences center.

5.4 Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we integrate, including:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics.
  • Stripe / PayPal: For secure payment processing.
  • Cloudflare: For security and performance.

5.5 Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings. You can:

  • Delete all cookies stored on your device.
  • Block all cookies (may affect site functionality).
  • Set preferences for specific sites.

For instructions, visit: AboutCookies.org

Refund, Renewal & Cancellation Policy

6.1 Refund Policy

  • Membership Fees: Membership fees are non-refundable once paid, except where required by applicable consumer protection laws.

  • Residency Bookings: Refunds for cancellations are governed by the Residency & Booking Policy (Section 4.3).
  • Olive Oil: Claims regarding quality or condition must be reported within 14 days of receipt.

6.2 Renewal Policy

  • Memberships renew automatically on the anniversary date.
  • Renewal notices will be sent via email 30 days before the renewal date.

  • Effect of Cancellation: Upon cancellation, all unused credits and allocations are forfeited.

Section 7

Olive Credits (OLC) Terms

7.1 Definition

Olive Credits ("OLC") are a digital representation of value within the Olivium ecosystem. OLC are not a cryptocurrency, security, or financial instrument. They have no monetary value outside the Olivium platform.

7.2 Earning OLC

  • Members receive OLC as part of their membership allocation.
  • OLC may be earned through participation in estate events, referrals, and community contributions.
  • OLC balances are displayed in the Olivium app.

7.3 Using OLC

  • OLC can be used to offset the cost of residency stays.
  • OLC can be used to purchase additional olive oil allocations.
  • OLC can be transferred to other members (subject to verification).

7.4 Expiry

  • OLC expire 24 months from the date of issuance.
  • Members will be notified before OLC expiry.
  • Expired OLC cannot be reinstated.

7.5 Limitations

  • OLC cannot be exchanged for cash.
  • OLC cannot be used for any purpose outside the Olivium ecosystem.
  • OLC are non-transferable upon membership termination.
