How Membership Works

Olivium operates as a limited private membership. The number of participants is intentionally capped to preserve the character of the land and the quality of experience.

Membership connects you to the estate through seasonal access, shared harvests, and time spent within the villa. Members receive annual stay allowances used to reserve time at the villa throughout the year.

Stays & Access

Stay allowances are renewed each season and can be used to book time at the villa. Availability is carefully managed to maintain a quiet, uncrowded environment.

The focus is not volume, but presence—time that feels unstructured, private, and grounded in nature.

Questions

Is this ownership of land?

No. Membership provides access and participation, not real estate ownership.

How are stays booked?

Members use their annual stay allowances to reserve time at the villa via a simple scheduling system.

Can I visit during harvest?

Yes. Members are invited to participate in seasonal moments, including the olive harvest.

Is membership transferable?

In some cases, memberships may be reassigned, subject to approval to preserve the character of the community.
