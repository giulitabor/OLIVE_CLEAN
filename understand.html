Frequently Shared Inquiries

Can I legally visit my assigned trees during the off-season?
+
Absolutely. Members simply log their intended arrival details inside the Olivium app platform so our grounds security and concierge teams can ensure private estate preparation.
What happens if a severe frost damages my designated sector trees?
+
Our reserve pool safety protocol completely shields your allocation. If emergency weather suppresses yield on your designated trees, your liter quota is completely fulfilled using equivalent backup reserves from our protected private family vault.
