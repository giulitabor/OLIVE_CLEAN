How Membership
Works

Olivium operates as a limited private membership. The number of participants is intentionally capped to preserve the character of the land.

01

Adopt

Your tree in the grove

Each member adopts a named, documented olive tree. Your tree is yours for the season — photographed, tended, and harvested on your behalf.

02

Stay

Use your villa nights

Members receive annual stay allowances to book quiet, private time at the villa. Availability is managed to keep the environment uncrowded.

03

Return

Each season, renewed

Membership is renewed with each harvest cycle. Members who return build a relationship with the land that deepens year over year.

Regenerative Stewardship

Land taken care of.

Every tree is documented, tended, and measured. We publish our environmental data each season.
