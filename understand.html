Founder Story

Born From a Belief That Land Should Be Lived

Olivium began with a single question: what if the people who cared most about a place could share in its stewardship? After years working across Tuscany's estate tourism and oil sector, our founder sought a model that was neither ownership nor tourism — but something richer.

"We wanted a place where returning felt like belonging. Not checking in — but coming home."

Now Olivium hosts members from across the world, united by a love for slow living, the land, and the ritual of harvest.

— Riccardo, Founder

The Beginning

Every Olive Begins as a Mignole

Membership Guide

Clear. Transparent. Designed For Life.

Olivium is designed to create a lifelong relationship with the estate. These principles explain how memberships work so every member knows exactly what to expect.

🌿

Stewardship, Not Ownership

Membership represents stewardship of the estate rather than legal ownership of land or olive trees. Members help preserve the grove while enjoying exclusive residency, harvest and olive oil privileges.

🌳

Your Olive Trees

Each named tree remains part of the historic Olivium grove while being stewarded by you throughout your active membership. Should one of your trees naturally decline over time, it will be respectfully replaced with another healthy tree so your stewardship continues uninterrupted.

🏡

Residency Credits

Residency Credits are your annual accommodation allowance. 1 Residency Credit = 1 complimentary overnight stay. Credits may be used consecutively or divided across multiple visits throughout the membership year.

👨‍👩‍👧

Guests

Residency Credits apply to the accommodation itself rather than per person. Members may stay with family or invited guests within the maximum occupancy of the reserved villa.

📅

Booking Priority

Higher membership tiers receive earlier booking windows for peak periods including harvest season, holidays and special estate events. All reservations remain subject to availability.

♻️

Unused Credits

Unused Residency Credits may roll over into the following membership year. To ensure fair access for every member, rollover credits are capped at one year's allowance. Example: If your membership includes 21 credits, your balance can never exceed 42.

🫒

Olive Oil Allocation

Every harvest produces premium extra virgin olive oil from the Olivium estate. Your annual allocation is reserved exclusively for you and may be collected during your stay or shipped where international delivery is available.

🍷

Estate Tastings

All members are welcome to enjoy olive oil tastings during estate visits. Private reserve tastings, guided pairings and limited harvest selections are exclusive experiences available to Grove Patrons and Full Tree Estate members.

🏛

Heritage Tree Dedication

Full Tree Estate members may dedicate one heritage olive tree with a permanent plaque celebrating their family or chosen name as part of the estate's ongoing story. This is separate from naming the trees included in your membership.

⭐

Founder Circle

Founder Circle recognition is available only during Olivium's founding membership period. Members joining during this period receive permanent Founder Circle recognition together with invitations to exclusive annual gatherings celebrating the estate's earliest supporters.

🤝

Estate Advisory Circle

Full Tree Estate members are invited to participate in annual advisory meetings where future estate experiences, sustainability initiatives and community projects are discussed. The Advisory Circle provides valuable input but does not constitute corporate governance or legal ownership.

✨

Concierge

Our concierge assists with residency planning, airport transfers, local dining reservations, winery visits, private chefs, wellness experiences and other estate services, allowing every visit to be tailored to your preferences.

Belong To Something That Grows Every Year

An Olivium membership is designed to become more meaningful with time. Every season brings another harvest, another return to Tuscany and another chapter in the story you build alongside one of Italy's living olive estates.

Frequently Asked

Questions

Is this ownership of land?
+
No. Membership provides access, participation and stewardship privileges, but does not constitute real-estate ownership. You participate in the estate, not own a share of the land.
Can I move my trees?
+
No. You cannot cut or move trees! Adopted Trees cannot be removed from the grove. You participate in the estate care, not destruction of the land.
How are stays reserved?
+
Members use their annual stay allowances through a simple reservation system with priority windows based on participation level. Grove Patrons and above receive early booking access each year.
Can I visit during harvest season?
+
Yes. Harvest season (October) is one of the most celebrated periods of the year and members are invited to participate in estate activities — from hand-picking to oil pressing and the harvest dinner.
Can membership be transferred?
+
In selected circumstances memberships may be reassigned, subject to approval to preserve the character and continuity of the community.
Why is the nightly rate lower than comparable villas?
+
Members participate in the stewardship model which allows accommodation costs to remain significantly below comparable luxury villa rates. Your membership contribution supports the estate's long-term management and olive production.
Do I need blockchain knowledge?
+
No. Membership and residency access are designed to be simple and accessible regardless of technical experience. The Olivium app handles all wallet and credit functionality in a familiar, intuitive interface.
What are Olive Credits (OLC)?
+
Olive Credits are the estate's internal credits earned through membership, participation, and harvests. They can be used to offset the cost of stays, purchase additional oil allocations, or transfer to other members.
What does Mignole mean?
+
Mignole is the Tuscan term for the tiny olive blossom that appears in spring. Each tree produces millions of mignole, each carrying the promise of a future olive. At Olivium, it's our symbol of potential, patience, and renewal.
